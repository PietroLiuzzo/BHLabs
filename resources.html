Online Resources

The Bibliotheca Hertziana maintains a number of online resources, tools and Databases. Some are curated by the Digital Humanities Lab, others made by the Library or Photo-library.

Fototeca Search

AI-powered image, gesture and iconography search through our 450,000 photos

3D Digital Library

Laser, relief and photogrammetry scanning our Rare Books collection

Virtual Reality

Architectural and exhibition reconstructions in 3 and 4 dimensions.

ZUCCARO

A rich graph-based semantic database of art and artists in Rome

CIPRO: Rome Maps

Extensive interactive catalogue of maps of Rome.

NOACK

Friedrich Noack's (1858-1930) digitised schedarium of artists in Rome.
